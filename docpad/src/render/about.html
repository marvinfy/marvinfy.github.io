About Marvin

My name is Marcus Vinicius Neves Silva (yeah, a big name - no pun intended). Most people know me as Marcus, but my family and a few friends call me Vini (English: Vinny). You can pick either one or call me by the nickname Marvin, a mash-up of the two ;)

I am a Brazilian-born, Canadian-hearted Software Developer living in the beautiful and peaceful city of St. John's, Newfoundland and Labrador.

I work for Rutter Inc. developing Radar Systems and am currently doing a Master of Science in Computer Science at Memorial University of Newfoundland. Whenever possible, I try and work on my side projects.

I love low-level software development in general, specially using C and C++, but my main interests are in Compilers, Process Virtual Machines, Radar Systems, and Distributed Systems. More recently, though, I've been also interested in mobile development using C# and The Xamarin Platform.
